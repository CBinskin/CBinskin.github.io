About Me!

Australian born and raised, I am an aspiring university graduate working towards a career in Communications and Media. As of March 2025, I have begun my journey completing my studies of the Diploma of Creative Industries and Communications with Western Sydney University - then a bachelor afterwards. I aspire to use the skills and knowledge I will be taught to develop a career in the Motorsports industry in Australia. Doing this, I hope to be a role model to young women also looking towards a career in Motorsports. Growing up with a family background in aviation, I understand the challenges and importance of staying committed and focused on your goals. My interest and passion for motorsports, has helped me strive to achieve my dream career and work with the amazing people within the industry.

Being a graduate, I’m looking to grow my understanding of the profession and how it operates. I would love to connect with others and learn about their own experiences. I’m open to learning about any requirements or recommendations before starting my journey, and any opportunities such as internships/work experience, tours and mentoring. - LinkedIn
